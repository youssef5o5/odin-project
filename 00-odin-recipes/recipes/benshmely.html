Ingredients

  • pasta
  • meat
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried mint
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of allspice
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger

steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the diced tomato and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the meat to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides.
  6. Season with salt, black pepper, cumin, paprika, dried mint, thyme, cinnamon, allspice, turmeric, ginger, and any other spices you prefer.
  7. Mix well and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes until the meat is cooked through and tender.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until well combined.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
